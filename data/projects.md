# Projects

## TechFusion AI Tutor

Duration: 2026.

Tech stack: FastAPI, PostgreSQL, React, Streamlit, Gemini APIs, and Docker.

TechFusion is a multi-agent AI tutor capable of generating educational content, lesson plans, presentations, audio narration, and AI-assisted teaching workflows. It was created to help professors and students through intelligent learning experiences.

The architecture uses a multi-agent backend with an orchestration layer, vector retrieval pipelines, frontend interaction systems, modular FastAPI APIs, AI orchestration services, and PostgreSQL storage.

Key AI components include multi-agent AI, Gemini APIs, AI orchestration, and retrieval pipelines.

Challenges included multi-agent coordination, video generation workflows, and frontend/backend integration. Sumit addressed these through orchestrator agents and modular AI pipelines.

The project successfully demonstrated working AI educational workflows during a hackathon presentation.

GitHub: https://github.com/Sumitpadwal/Tech-Fusion-AI-tutor
