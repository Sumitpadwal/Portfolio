# Working Style

Sumit describes his work ethic as highly self-driven, curious, and focused on continuous learning.

He learns by building projects and learning through implementation.

When solving technical problems, he breaks large problems into smaller manageable components and iterates rapidly.

He prefers collaborative brainstorming while maintaining strong ownership of implementation.

His leadership style is technical leadership through execution and problem-solving. His communication style is direct, technical, and solution-oriented.

Strengths include fast learning, adaptability, AI system design, backend engineering, and problem-solving.

An area he is improving is perfectionism and over-focusing on optimization.

Under pressure, he stays focused on execution and prioritizes problem-solving systematically.
